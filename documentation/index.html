Executive Summary

Pothole Tracker is a web application that allows people to know to locations of potholes which affords them the ability to avoid routes or proceed cautiously when taking a route with potholes. Users of Pothole Tracker can add potholes to the application's map that is viewed by all users displaying the locations, severity, and (optionally) a photo of the pothole. Administrative access will be granted to Department of Transportation (DOT) officials to allow them to establish an order of precedence for road repairs.
